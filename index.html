Who We Are

RIEN Hub is the innovation arm of RIEN X (Reliable Intelligent Ecosystem Network) — combining mobility, technology, and creativity to build the next era of connected ecosystems. We are a collective of engineers, designers, and visionaries headquartered in Malaysia, driven by a singular goal: to make smart, sustainable technology accessible and human-driven.

Our Vision

To create a smart, sustainable, and connected digital ecosystem that empowers lives globally.

Our Mission

Unite innovators, engineers, and creators to design solutions that connect technology with purpose.
